Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a hearty, slow-simmered pasta dish that brings together the deep, savory flavors of a classic meat ragù with the comfort of perfectly cooked spaghetti. Originating from Bologna, Italy (where it’s traditionally served with tagliatelle), the dish has become a global favorite—an emblem of warmth, richness, and home-cooked satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce
  • Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Garlic
